Challenge Report, PairUAV: Last-Meter Precision Navigation for UAVs
(Optional)
The submission website is at https://www.codabench.org/competitions/15251/. (registered with
your student ID or your name).
This year’s challenge focuses on **fine-grained last-meter UAV navigation**. Given a source
image and a target image (PairUAV dataset derived from University-1652), participants are required
to predict the relative angle (heading) and distance (translation) needed to align the UAV with the
target pose.
Since the course ends early, the deadline for course credit is April 30, 2026 at 23:59 PM. Please
take a screenshot of your best result on the CodaBench leaderboard and submit it with a short
technical report (e.g., which model do you use?) on UMoodle by this date.
You are strongly encouraged to continue improving your model and submit to the official Cod-
aBench competition until the final deadline (June 30, 2026 at 23:59 AoE) for the workshop awards.
Dataset & Submission:
• Request the University-1652 dataset and generate the PairUAV training set using the provided
script (usually replied within 5 minutes).
• Submission: Upload a zip file containing only result.txt at the root.
• Each line in result.txt should contain: angle, distance (e.g., 120.5, 35.8).
• The number of lines must exactly match the test set.
